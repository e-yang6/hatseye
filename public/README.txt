Sound Files for HATSEYE

Place your MP3 sound files in this folder:

1. wake_word_sound.mp3
   - Plays when the wake word "hey hats eye" is detected
   - Recommended: A short, pleasant chime or notification tone (0.5-1 second)

2. question_received_sound.mp3
   - Plays when a question is successfully received after wake word detection
   - Recommended: A short, pleasant acknowledgment sound (0.5-1 second)

Note: These files are optional. If the files don't exist or can't be loaded, the application will continue without playing sounds (no errors).
